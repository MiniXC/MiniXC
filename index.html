When is a side project done?

I want this question to be easy to answer, but it usually really isn't.
This leads to me having the following cycle of neverending and multiplying side projects.

get exited about an idea


put in X hours to make it work


... oh, is that another, shinier idea?


Defintion of Done

From the time of writing (May 2020) on, I want to avoid this cycle.
When I am ready to call a side project done, or want to move on to something else, I will post about it here. 

I hope this works out.
